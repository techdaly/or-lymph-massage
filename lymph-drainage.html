Welcome to Oregon Lymphatics & Massage

Janelle Cline, RN, LMT

Specializing in Lymph Drainage Therapy, Lymphatic Breast Care, Craniosacral Therapy, and Integrative Massage.

Enjoy a massage with Lymph Drainage Therapy (LDT) and Craniosacral Therapy (CST). LDT is a modality that goes beyond muscle massage and addresses swelling and inflammation. CST is a light touch that releases restrictions in the tissue layers surrounding the brain and spinal column. These modalities are combined with wonderful, feel good massage which leads to a blissful experience.
