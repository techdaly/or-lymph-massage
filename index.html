Welcome to Oregon Lymphatics & Massage

Janelle Cline, RN, LMT

Specializing in Lymph Drainage Therapy, Lymphatic Breast Care, Craniosacral Therapy, Myofascial Release (MFR), and Integrative Massage, and Certified in Manual Lymph Drainage (MLD).

Enjoy a massage with Lymph Drainage Therapy (LDT) and Craniosacral Therapy (CST). LDT is a modality that goes beyond muscle massage and addresses swelling and inflammation. CST is a light touch that releases restrictions in the tissue layers surrounding the brain and spinal column. These modalities are combined with wonderful, feel good massage including Myofascial Release for a blissful experience.
